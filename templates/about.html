Origin Story

I have been reading and collecting comics since I was 5 years old. In addition to the amazing journey from the stories in the comics, this hobby has been a great way to meet and connect with a fantastic comic enthusiast community

I always enjoyed buying comics and trading them as my interests in collecting changed. While comic stores and online auction platforms are great, in order to get the comics I wanted, I would have to sell or trade them with fees that ranged from 10-20+% of the comic value and lose that valuable equity in those books.

Thus, theComicSwap.com was born.

The Comic Swap provides fellow collectors the assurance and confidence that there is a third-party resource to provide a guarantee that both parties will have the trade verified and insure that what you want is what you receive.

As the first website to validate, insure, and provide a guarantee that the trades are correct, I am confident that this resource will provide you with the best professional experience for comic trading.

Enjoy the collecting journey!
